Things to Do in Silicon Valley

  • Catch a bite to eat at the historic restaurant John's Grill, which prepares excellent steaks and expertly prepared fresh seafood.
  • Experience an up close and personal musical setting at Feinstein's at the Nikko, where a selection of Broadway, jazz, and cabaret music is performed by local musicians daily.
  • Yerba Buena Center for the Arts offers contemporary selections of art exhibitions, featuring local artists and community based works.
  • For classic Irish and international dishes, visit Johnny Foley's which offers classic rock n' roll cover bands as well as Dueling Pianos.
  • Dine on authentic Italian cuisine in the beautiful restaurant Kuletos, which features unique meals and an alluring interior.
  • Kin Khao serves declicious Thai food that is exquisitely handcrafted to be as genuine as possible.
  • Looking for a round of golf? Presidio Golf Course & Clubhouse is award-winning and established as a challenging yet stunning golf course.
  • Illuminate SF is an innovative embrace of modern San Francisco creativity; self-guided tours are offered to visit all the brilliant light art exhibits SF has to offer.
